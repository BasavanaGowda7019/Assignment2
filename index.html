Who we are

Adopt-A-Pals is a non-profit animal welfare organization focused on rescuing, rehabilitating and rehoming abandoned animals. Every pet that enters our center receives medical care, vaccinations and a behavior assessment before being matched with a family.

What we do

  • Rescue & medical care for injured or abandoned animals.
  • Pet adoption & foster placement programs.
  • Vaccination, microchipping & sterilization drives.
  • Community education on responsible pet care.

How adoption works

A simple and transparent process centered around the wellbeing of the animal and your family.

  1. 1. Browse & enquire

    Explore available pets online or visit the shelter. Submit an interest form for the animals you connect with.

  2. 2. Meet & match

    Our team arranges a meet-and-greet and helps check compatibility with your lifestyle and home.

  3. 3. Home check & paperwork

    We conduct a quick home check, complete the legal paperwork and share medical records and care instructions.

  4. 4. Welcome home!

    Your new pal comes home. Our team supports you with post-adoption guidance whenever needed.
